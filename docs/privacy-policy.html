Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Overview

RemoteCTRL is a self-hosted remote access tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data through our servers.

Data Collection

RemoteCTRL does not collect any data. All connections are direct between your device and your own server. We have no servers that process your data.

Network Connections

RemoteCTRL connects to:

  • Your server — via SSH/SFTP for terminal and file management
  • Cloudflare — for secure tunneling (if you enable remote access). Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to tunnel traffic.
  • Apple Push Notification Service — to deliver push notifications to your device

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, your device's Expo push token is stored on your own server. This token is used solely to send you notifications about session events and server health. You can disable push notifications at any time in Settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare Tunnel — used for remote access. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Expo Push Notification Service — used for delivering push notifications. Expo Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

If we change this privacy policy, we will update the date above and post the revised policy here.
